Chimp Tag Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10/19/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data while you use Chimp Tag.

1. Data We Collect

Microphone Access: We use your microphone to enable voice chat only during multiplayer sessions. Mirror VR provides this functionality. We do not access or record your microphone data outside the app.

Player Information: We collect and maintain your in-game name, Oculus username, and ban status to ensure moderation and persistence across devices.

Cosmetics Ownership: We store data about cosmetics you own so they remain available across sessions.

Device Information: We collect limited device info (like your device ID) to save and link your data. All data is securely stored via PlayFab.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide Mirror VR voice chat and multiplayer features.
  • To enforce bans and maintain safety.
  • To keep cosmetics and identity consistent across devices.
  • To ensure smooth data sync with PlayFab.

3. Your Rights & Choices

You may request deletion of your data by contacting myaltacc337@gmail.com.

Include your in-game name and either cosmetics owned or PlayFab ID for verification.
